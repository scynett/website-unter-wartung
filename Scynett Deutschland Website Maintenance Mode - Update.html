About Us

Scynett offers IT solutions to organizations from the analysis phase through to the development and maintenance Phase.We involve our customers at every stage of development to ensure the best satisfaction of the end product.

We build cross-platform desktop applications. Our agile approach to software development as well as the uses of established design patterns and architectures ensures that your application remains extensible and easily testable. We always thrive to build modern user-friendly applications to ease the time needed to get accustomed to their new applications.

We build state of the art mobile apps. Our expertise in IOS, Android and Windows app development protects our clients from unpleasant surprises. We support you throughout the project period; from conception through the app programming to the submission of the App to the various App stores.

There are several technologies that enable already existing applications communicate with other application within or outside your network. At Scynett we help our customers by analyzing their existing environments and coming up with cost-effective solutions achieving desired results. Talk to us today and we guarantee to find the right solution to your problem.
